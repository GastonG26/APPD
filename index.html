The Accountable Purpose Framework

Set goals. Check in weekly.
Grow the whole person.

Students set goals across four areas of their lives and check in on those goals weekly — building self-awareness and accountability as they work toward what matters most to them.

P
Physical

The body as a foundation — sleep, nutrition, movement, and recovery as prerequisites for everything else.

I
Intellectual

Goal-setting, academic habits, and engaging with learning as a personal mission — not just a grade.

E
Emotional

Naming feelings, managing pressure, and developing the emotional vocabulary to navigate real life.

S
Social

Leadership, accountability to community, and the relational skills that outlast any season or semester.

The Experience

Workshop-style.
Discussion-driven.

Not a lecture — a guided experience. The same format used at the University of Texas Sports and Social Work program, where participants discover the framework through conversation and leave with real goals attached.

01

Self-Assessment

Participants rate themselves across all four areas on a Likert scale and explain their score — the same check-in students will use going forward. Honest, not graded.

02

Framework Introduction

The AP model unpacked through stories and discussion — what it looks like in practice, why the four areas work together, and how to use it daily.

03

SMART Goal-Setting

Every participant leaves with at least one written SMART goal tied to one of the four Accountable Purpose areas — and a real accountability structure, not just a good intention.

04

Pulse Check Platform Intro

Participants explore the app that extends the workshop beyond a single session — routine check-ins, AI coaching, and visual progress tracking over time.

05

Implementation Planning

For adult cohorts: a working plan for bringing the framework into their school, team, or program — with the tools and follow-up to make it stick.

The Platform

Pulse Check keeps growth
going after the workshop.

One workshop plants the seed. Pulse Check is the daily practice that grows it — a proactive mental health and accountability platform coaches and students use together.

Immediate, tailored feedback after every check-in — ensuring no student falls through the cracks between conversations.
